  • I have chosen the most appropriate communication channel
  • I have gathered all necessary information
  • I understand any cultural considerations
  • I have considered the recipient's perspective

  • My message is clear and concise

  • I have proofread for errors
  • Action items and deadlines are clearly stated
  • Contact information is provided

üì§ Delivery Phase

  • I have confirmed recipient contact details
